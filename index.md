## About Me

I am currently supervised by [Jiatao Gu](https://jiataogu.me/). Fortunately, I work as a research intern in the [VLAA lab](https://ucsc-vlaa.github.io/) at the University of California, Santa Cruz, supervised by [Yuyin Zhou](https://yuyinzhou.github.io/) and [Juncheng Wu](https://chtholly17.github.io/). My research interests lie in computer vision and multi-modal large language models! I also work as Deputy Technical Director in a startup [FingerDance](https://fingerdance.ai/home/).
